Lonelier Pong

 

 

 

 

 

 

Goal

 

Goal of the game is to bounce the ball back and forth indefinitely. There is no score to display your progress, as I preferred few lines of code over a competitive gaming experience.

 

 

 

 

Controls

 

  • Left arrow: Put paddle at left side
  • Right arrow: Move paddle towards the right

 

 

 

 

About

 

LonelierPong is a 1 player pong game, written in Processing.

 

The name and gameplay of LonelierPong is inspired on Thijs van Beers' smash hit LonelyPong.
