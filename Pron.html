Gameplay

 

Goal is to make the other play bump into a non-black pixel.

 

The world is donut-shaped.

 

 

 

 

 

Controls

 

  • a: blue player turn left
  • d: blue player turn right
  • arrow left: red player turn left
  • arrow right: red player turn right
