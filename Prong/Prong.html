Prong

 

 

 

 

 

 

Gameplay

 

Prong ia cooperative Pong game.

Players 1 and 3 control the upper red paddle. Players 2 and 4 control the lower blue paddle.

Goal is to never let the ball pass your team's paddle, and to get the ball to pass the other team's paddle.

 

 

 

 

 

Controls

 

  • a: player 1 left
  • s: player 1 right
  • h: player 2 left
  • j: player 2 right
  • ': player 3 left
  • \: player 3 right
  • arrow left: player 4 left
  • arrow right: player 4 right
